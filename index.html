What is Meshtastic?

An open-source technology that turns pocket-sized LoRa radios into a long-range, decentralized, and encrypted mesh network. With no SIM cards, internet, or monthly fees, devices relay messages to one another entirely off-grid.

Explore Meshtastic Project

Our Mission

MutiaraMesh provides a free, resilient fallback communication network for Penang. By connecting home nodes and solar-powered hilltop repeaters, we ensure Penangites can reach loved ones when normal infrastructure fails.
